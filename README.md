# hodosme 🛤️

*A natural language seen at its deepest time-scale — a collective walk of countless speakers over generations toward the realm of meaning, received as gift before anyone alive adds a word to it.*

hodosme is two true roots welded into one word: the Greek **hodos** (ὁδός — way, path, road, journey), the trail that runs from the Sacred Way to Eleusis through Plato's path out of the cave to the early Christians who named themselves "the Way," and the Sumerian **me** (𒈨), the eldest word for a fundamental that was *received*, not made. So hodosme is a walked-path held as ordinance: every tongue is the deposit of an enormous collective walk, handed to its speakers before any of them contribute their own small footstep. Homer's Greek is not the Koine is not today's demotic; the language anyone finds is the trail-so-far.

Where synchronic linguistics describes a language frozen at one moment, hodosme names the long process underneath — the diachronic frame within which a single grasp (noesisme) or a single confirmed joy (eurekame) becomes possible. Eleven-plus traditions independently walked this structure into vocabulary (*hodos*, *halakhah*, *mārga*, *dào*, *sabīl*, *via*…), and hodosme generalizes it: under THE-PATH's reading, every natural language is such a path. Its light is that it makes inheritance and custody sayable, and a thinning heritage-tongue namable; its shadow is the fatalism that makes the living speaker small, or the relic-worship that treats all change as decay. Forged in the cathedral at weighted **8.05/10** — the first canon entry born under THE-PATH.
